    Melinna Nkomo is a dynamic graphic designer based in Johannesburg, Gauteng, known for her creativity and strong visual communication skills. With a Bachelor of Arts from Rhodes University, she has honed her ability to translate complex ideas into compelling designs. Currently serving as a Junior Graphic Designer at JSM Pipe Project, Melinna provides bespoke design services that encompass branding, logo creation, and digital assets. She thrives in collaborative environments, working closely with clients to understand their vision and deliver outstanding results. Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite and skilled in UX/UI design, she continuously seeks out new challenges and stays updated with the latest design trends, making her a valuable asset in the graphic design field.
